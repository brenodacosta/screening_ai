# Delivery Driver — Grupos Sazón

Grupos Sazón is a restaurant chain operating across 45 cities in Spain and Mexico. We're hiring delivery drivers to bring food from our kitchens to our customers — directly, with no third-party platform in between.

## Role

You'll be assigned orders from the kitchen closest to you. The driver app handles routing, customer notifications, and proof-of-delivery. Most shifts are 4–8 hours and you pick them in the app a week in advance.

## A typical day

- Sign in at your chosen start time.
- The app assigns 4–8 orders per hour depending on demand.
- You pick up at the kitchen, drive to the customer, mark delivered.
- Tips are settled to your account at end-of-shift.

## What we look for

- Reliability (you show up to the shifts you pick).
- Friendly attitude with customers and kitchen staff.
- Care with the food — bags sealed, drinks upright, temperature kept.

## What we don't ask for here

We don't ask for ID numbers, bank details, or addresses during this short screening — only what's needed to know if you're eligible. Full onboarding documents come after a recruiter contacts you.

## FAQs

**¿Cuánto se gana? / How much do I earn?**
Hourly base plus 100% of tips. Spain: €9–€13/hour base. Mexico: MXN 60–95/hour base. Tips on top.

**¿Necesito moto o coche propio? / Do I need my own vehicle?**
Yes for most zones. Some city centers operate bike-only routes — no motor-vehicle license required there.

**¿Pago semanal? / Weekly pay?**
Bi-weekly direct deposit. Tips clear daily into the in-app wallet.

**¿Puedo elegir horarios? / Can I choose shifts?**
Yes — you pick a week in advance from the available slots.

**¿Cuándo puedo empezar? / How soon can I start?**
Most candidates start within 5–10 days of recruiter contact, after document verification.
